Meet the Team

The people behind Northern Ireland Music Therapy Trust

Our team brings together therapists, staff, and trustees who are passionate about improving lives through music therapy. We work collaboratively to support children, young people, adults, older people, families, and partner organisations across Northern Ireland.

Chief Executive Officer:

Music Therapy Manager
