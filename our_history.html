Our History

OUR STORY

Northern Ireland Music Therapy Trust began with a simple but powerful vision: to make music therapy more accessible to people across Northern Ireland who could benefit from it most.

In the early years, the focus was on building awareness, establishing trust, and creating the foundation for a service that could grow sustainably over time.

1990
Growth

EXPANDING REACH

As awareness of music therapy grew, the Trust began supporting a wider range of client groups and building stronger relationships with schools, healthcare settings, and community organisations.

This period was shaped by steady development, stronger partnerships, and a clearer public understanding of the impact music therapy can have on communication, wellbeing, and quality of life.

2003
Impact

BUILDING IMPACT

By this stage, Northern Ireland Music Therapy Trust had developed a stronger presence across multiple service areas, including mental health, neurological support, dementia care, and children’s services.

The organisation’s work increasingly focused on delivering meaningful outcomes while continuing to build evidence, trust, and long-term partnerships.

2012
Today

LOOKING FORWARD

Today, the Trust continues to evolve, reaching more people through therapy, partnerships, research-informed practice, and improved digital communication.

With a renewed focus on accessibility, storytelling, and service visibility, the next chapter is about helping more people discover the support music therapy can offer.

2025

Supporting people across Northern Ireland through compassionate, evidence-informed music therapy.
